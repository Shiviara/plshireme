About me

I’m Marta, a Multimedia Design student in Business Academy Aarhus. I’m calm and open-minded. I dislike not understanding something, so I’m always willing to learn. And according to some people I’m also the most organised person they know.

How I work

As a UX/UI designer I’m... Ideating, wireframing and prototyping are my favourite steps in the process. I can work independently but I love working in a team. I believe that combining minds of multiple people always provides better, broaden results. I thrive in combining ideas of all the team-members into solid solutions. Testing is my best friend! It helps with catching mistakes early in the process, resolves conflicts in a team and immensely improves project in general.
