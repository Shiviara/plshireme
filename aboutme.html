About me

I’m Marta, a Multimedia Design student in Business Academy Aarhus. I’m hard-working and always willing to learn something new. In my spare time you can find me attending different events or volunteering in Café Mellemfolk. I’m facing the world with a calm and down-to-earth attitude and a pinch of optimism. According to some people, I’m also the most organised person they know.

How I work

  • Ideating, wireframing and prototyping are my favourite steps in the process.
  • I can work independently but I love working in a team. I believe that combining minds of multiple people always provides better, broaden results.
  • I thrive in combining ideas of all the team-members and building upon them while brainstorming.
  • Testing is my second favourite part of the process. It brings problems into the surface, solves team conflicts and inspires for searching for better solutions.
